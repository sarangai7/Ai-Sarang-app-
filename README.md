# AI Sarang - Your Universal Daily Life Solver

**Progressive Web App (PWA)** built with **zero investment** using only free tools.

## 🎯 What is AI Sarang?

AI Sarang is a simple, practical and safe AI-powered app that helps anyone solve everyday problems quickly.

It works directly in your browser and can be installed on your phone like a real native app (no app store needed).

## ✨ Key Features

- Ask any daily life question in simple English
- Get instant, practical, safe and **zero-cost** solutions
- Works **offline** (PWA support)
- Clean, modern and mobile-first ultra HD design
- No login or registration required
- Completely free to use

## 📱 Categories

- 💰 Money Saving & Budget Tips
- ❤️ Health & Fitness Advice
- 📚 Learning & Skill Development
- 🍳 Food Recipes with Available Ingredients
- 🧠 Mental Wellness & Stress Relief
- 💼 Career & Job Guidance
- 🌱 Environment & Daily Sustainability

## How to Use

1. Open the link above
2. Type your question (example: "How can I save money with low income?")
3. Get instant practical AI solution
4. Tap "Add to Home Screen" to install it like a real app
